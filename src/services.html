Our Services

We offer a full range of title and escrow services to ensure a smooth and secure closing process for every real estate transaction in Montezuma and Dolores Counties.

Title Insurance

Title insurance is a critical component of any real estate transaction. It protects you from financial loss due to defects in the title. Our experienced team performs a thorough title search to identify any potential issues, such as liens, encumbrances, or errors in public records, and we issue a policy that protects your investment for as long as you own the property.

Escrow & Closing Services

Our role as a neutral third party is to facilitate the closing process, ensuring all parties meet their obligations in a timely and professional manner. We handle all the details, including document preparation, fund disbursement, and recording the new deed. We are committed to providing a friendly, no-stress closing experience for buyers, sellers, and agents.
